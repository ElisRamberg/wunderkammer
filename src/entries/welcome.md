A *Wunderkammer* — a "cabinet of curiosities" — was a room where people kept
the interesting things they collected before museums existed: shells, fossils,
instruments, oddities, all jumbled together. That mix is the point.

This site works the same way. It's a **notebook that can grow up**: somewhere to
drop a project, an experiment, a piece of writing, or a half-formed idea without
much ceremony. Nothing here has to be finished.

Every entry shares the same outer frame — the nav, the type label, and a little
**status badge** (idea → prototype → working → polished) so you can tell how
baked something is. Underneath that frame, each entry is free to be whatever it
needs to be.
